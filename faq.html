Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Tyanhelp?

Tyanhelp is a community-driven platform that connects individuals looking to adopt pets with shelters and rescue organizations.

How can I contact support?

You can reach us through the contact form on our website or by emailing support@tyanhelp.org.

Adoption Questions

What is the adoption process?

The adoption process involves filling out an application, meeting with the pet, and completing an adoption contract.

Are there adoption fees?

Yes, there are adoption fees which vary depending on the organization and the animal.

Volunteer Questions

How can I volunteer?

You can volunteer by filling out a volunteer application form on our website.

What types of volunteer opportunities are available?

We have various opportunities including event support, animal care, and administrative tasks.
